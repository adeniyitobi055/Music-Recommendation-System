Music Recommendation System

  • Start now

Having to search for music is tiring and stressful coupled with the fact that we have millions if not billions of music available for us to listen to. However, not all fall into the category of songs we love to listen to especially when we need to rest, vibe, and sometimes cool of from a frustrating event or day. My team and I decided to design a system that'll eliminate the stress of having to search for a particular song when it's really needed.

This system does not eliminate song search by the user. However, it seives the result to suit the user's needs by taking into account the song that have been listened to by other users and recommending the ones that are closely related to the song entered by the user. The system also recommends songs that have high listening counts - which are also referred to as trending music.
